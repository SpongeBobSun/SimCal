SimCal

Simple calendar APP for android.
SimCal provide a calendar view and time line for specific date.
You can add & delete & modify events or todos in SimCal.
SimCal also provide notifications for events & todos.
To be honest I'm not sure what this app will looks like finally. I wrote it just for fun :)

Screenshots are below

Click add button to add an event.

Click date cell to change date view. Selected date's timeline & events will be displayed.

"Today View" will show all events which occures today.
